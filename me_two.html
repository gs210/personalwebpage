fave restaurant

Three Broomsticks

My favorite restaurant recently has been the Three Broomsticks. It is located in the Hogsmeade section of the Wizarding World of Harry Potter and is a replica of the pub from the Harry Potter movies. Three Broomsticks offers a wide selection of traditional British dishes such as shepherd’s pie and fish and chips, along with butterbeer.

What makes it my favorite restaurant is the immersive atmosphere, since every detail makes you feel like you have stepped into the wizarding world. Eating there feels less like a regular meal and more like an experience, which is why it stands out as my favorite.

fave food

Sweet Potato Pie

My favorite food is my family’s sweet potato pie. In southern North Carolina, my grandmother would always serve this delicacy at family gatherings, and it quickly became something I associated with comfort and southern hospitality.

Though I live far away from my mom’s side of the family now, I still make it as much as possible in New England.

The warm mashed sweet potatoes, caramelized brown sugar, and blend of spices in the pie pair nicely when served with a dollop of whipped cream on top.
